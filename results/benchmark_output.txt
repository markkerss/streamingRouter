=================================================================

RNA sequencing (RNA-seq) is a powerful technology that allows for the analysis of gene expression across many different samples. However, the resulting data is sensitive and must be protected to prevent unauthorized access. To address this problem, we have developed [IsoRatio](https://missmarmalade.github.io/IsoRatio.js/), a deep learning algorithm that generates synthetic RNA-seq data while preserving meaningful biological properties. You can try it out at [this link](https://missmarmalade.github.io/IsoRatio.js/).

How to Use IsoRatio
--------------------

To use IsoRatio, simply upload your RNA-seq data (preferably accompanied by corresponding samples metadata) to our web application along with any relevant parameter settings you would like to use. You can adjust the parameters using the sliders and text fields provided. Once you have configured your settings, click the "Generate Synthetic Data" button to receive your synthetic data file.
